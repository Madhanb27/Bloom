# Bloom 🌿 — Project Rulebook

## 1. Project Overview

**Project Name:** Bloom  
**Type:** Premium 3D scroll-animated marketing website  
**Industry:** Plants, home decor, lifestyle, e-commerce  
**Goal:** Create a premium portfolio website concept with a strong 3D hero animation, warm visual direction, and luxury botanical brand feel.

Bloom is a fictional premium plant and home decor brand that sells indoor plants, ceramic pots, decor objects, and gift sets for modern homes.

The website should feel calm, warm, organic, elegant, and premium.

---

## 2. Brand Positioning

Bloom is not just a plant store. It is a premium lifestyle brand that brings nature into modern living spaces.

**Core idea:**  
Bringing nature, warmth, and calm into modern homes through curated plants and decor.

**Brand keywords:**
- Warm
- Organic
- Premium
- Calm
- Elegant
- Minimal
- Sophisticated
- Natural
- Editorial
- Timeless

---

## 3. Target Audience

Bloom is designed for people who care about beautiful homes, calm interiors, and premium decor.

**Primary users:**
- Young professionals
- Home decor lovers
- Apartment owners
- Interior styling enthusiasts
- Gift buyers
- Premium lifestyle shoppers

---

## 4. Visual Direction

Bloom should look like a luxury botanical lifestyle brand.

The design should feel:
- Warm and cozy
- Clean and spacious
- Premium but not flashy
- Organic and natural
- Editorial and elegant
- Product-photography inspired

The website should use soft backgrounds, large whitespace, calm animations, and premium 3D product visuals.

**Warm Cream**  
`#F7F3EE`

**Sage Green**  
`#6F8B74`  
Used for buttons, accents, links, active states, and brand highlights.

### Secondary Neutral
**Soft Beige**  
`#D9C8B4`  
Used for cards, product containers, subtle backgrounds, and ceramic-inspired surfaces.

### Accent Color
**Terracotta**  
`#C97A56`  
Used sparingly for decorative highlights, hover states, and warm visual accents.

### Text Color
**Charcoal**  
`#2B2B2B`

### Luxury Accent
**Soft Gold**  
`#D8B26E`  
Used only in small amounts for premium highlights, dividers, glow effects, and decorative details.

### Final Palette
```text
Warm Cream   #F7F3EE
Sage Green   #6F8B74
Soft Beige   #D9C8B4
Terracotta   #C97A56
Charcoal     #2B2B2B
Soft Gold    #D8B26E
```

### Color Usage Rules
- Use Warm Cream as the dominant background.
- Use Sage Green as the main brand accent.
- Use Soft Beige for soft containers and product areas.
- Use Terracotta only as a small warm accent.
- Use Soft Gold very carefully for premium glow and highlight moments.
- Avoid bright, neon, cold, or overly saturated colors.

## 6. Typography

### Heading Font
**Cormorant Garamond**

Used for:
- Hero headings
- Section titles
- Large editorial statements
- Premium marketing copy

Mood:
- Elegant
- Editorial
- Premium
- Soft
- Timeless

### Body / UI Font
**Inter**

Used for:
- Paragraphs
- Navigation
- Buttons
- Forms
- Product descriptions
- Footer text

Mood:
- Clean
- Modern
- Readable
- Minimal

### Typography Hierarchy
```text
H1: Cormorant Garamond, large, elegant, high contrast
H2: Cormorant Garamond, spacious, editorial
H3: Cormorant Garamond or Inter Medium depending on section
Body: Inter Regular
Buttons: Inter Medium
Navigation: Inter Medium
Captions: Inter Regular / Small
```

### Typography Rules
- Keep headings short and elegant.
- Use generous line height.
- Avoid crowded paragraphs.
- Body text should be simple and easy to scan.
- Buttons should use calm wording.

Good button examples:
```text
Shop Collection
Explore Plants
View Products
Join Newsletter
```

Avoid:
```text
SHOP NOW!!!
BUY TODAY
CLICK HERE
LIMITED OFFER!!!
```

## 7. Design Principles

Bloom should follow these design principles:

1. **One strong focal point per section**  
   Avoid too many competing visuals.

2. **Premium whitespace**  
   Use large spacing and breathing room.

3. **Calm over flashy**  
   Animations should feel smooth and organic.

4. **Editorial layout**  
   The website should feel like a premium lifestyle magazine.

5. **Natural materials**  
   Use ceramic, stone, clay, wood, linen, and foliage-inspired visuals.

6. **Soft visual hierarchy**  
   Avoid harsh contrast unless needed for readability.

---

## 9. Lighting Rules

All visuals should use warm, soft, premium lighting.

Preferred lighting:
- Soft studio lighting
- Warm sunlight
- Golden hour glow
- Gentle shadows
- Volumetric sunlight rays
- Subtle spotlight from above

Avoid:
- Harsh shadows
- Dark moody lighting
- Cyberpunk lighting
- RGB lighting
- Cold blue lighting

---

## 10. Photography and Render Style

The visual style should feel like luxury product photography mixed with premium 3D rendering.

Render style:
- Photorealistic
- Ultra clean
- Premium product-shot quality
- Warm cream studio background
- Soft realistic shadows
- High-end botanical advertising aesthetic

Do not create visuals that feel like:
- Stock photos
- Low-quality AI renders
- Cartoonish 3D
- Gaming assets
- Cheap e-commerce images

## 11. Hero Asset — Approved Direction

### Hero Concept
A single floating Monstera Deliciosa plant in a premium matte beige ceramic planter.

### Hero Visual Rules
- Single plant only
- Floating in mid-air
- Centered composition
- Warm cream background
- Soft shadow underneath
- Luxury studio lighting
- Premium botanical product photography feel
- No furniture
- No room scene
- No extra objects
- No text, labels, or logos inside the image

The plant is calm, centered, and floating still in a warm cream studio environment.

### End Frame
The plant becomes radiant and alive with golden sunlight rays, floating botanical particles, and gently expanded leaves.

### Hero Motion
- Calm plant → blooming radiant plant → calm plant
- Organic leaf movement
- Warm volumetric sunlight
- Floating particles
- Ultra slow motion
- Seamless loop

## 12. Motion System

Bloom's motion should feel alive, slow, and organic.

Allowed motion styles:
- Ultra slow motion
- Floating objects
- Breathing movement
- Gentle leaf sway
- Sunlight bloom
- Soft particles
- Smooth parallax
- Slow image reveal
- Seamless loops

Avoid:
- Fast cuts
- Explosions
- Aggressive movement
- Glitch effects
- Tech transitions
- Shaky camera
- Overly dramatic motion

## 14. Video Prompt Formula

Use this structure for future Bloom animated assets:

```text
Start Frame → End Frame
Video 1: Calm state to radiant state
Video 2: Radiant state to calm state
Merge both videos into a seamless loop
```

### Motion Prompt Style
The prompt should describe one continuous cinematic transformation.

Example structure:
```text
A premium [object] floats perfectly still in mid-air against a warm cream studio background.
Very slowly, [organic transformation happens].
Warm sunlight appears, soft particles drift, and the object becomes more alive.
The motion remains elegant, cinematic, ultra slow, and premium.
No camera shake.
```
